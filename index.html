Frequently Ask Question

Who is PolicyVilla?

PolicyVilla is based in Delhi, India. Our mission, is to render best of our services in providing you a platform where you can meet the Insurance providers and Brokers/Agents, helping busy consumers make better Insurance decisions, while maintaining a high level of customer service.
policyvilla.com allows customers to search for the plan that suits their requirement the most. We are insurer agnostic and have tie-ups with most of the insurance companies operating in India. We bring to your doorstep- information and comparisons based on key features and prices of different insurers for Life insurance, Term Life insurance, Health insurance, Car insurance, Travel insurance etc.

Is it cheaper to buy insurance through PolicyVilla.com?

Insurance is a regulated market. No insurance company, neither PolicyVilla nor any other agent can give you a deal cheaper than what the insurance company has set. It is illegal to do so. The best way to save money on insurance premiums is by comparing various insurance plans available and making an informed choice.

What is an insurance aggregator?

An insurance aggregator is an online resource with a search utility that facilitates the user to get insurance quotes from many companies and allows comparison between them. A good aggregator equips you with enough information to be able to pick the most suitable insurance for your purpose.

How safe is my information with PolicyVilla?

Your privacy is totally safe with PolicyVilla. Please refer to our Privacy Policy for details.

Does PolicyVilla honor my claim?

Since PolicyVilla is not an insurance company; we cannot honor your claim. You need to present the claim to your insurance provider. Although as facilitators, we can guide you with the step by step procedure for making a claim
